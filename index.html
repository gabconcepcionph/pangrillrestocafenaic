Welcome to Pan Grill

About PAN GRILL Resto + Cafe — Rooftop Dining in Naic

Perched atop the Overland Building on Governor's Drive in Naic, Cavite, PAN GRILL Resto + Cafe is a charming rooftop destination that blends savory meals with a relaxed café vibe. Open daily from 8 AM to 10:30 PM, it's the perfect spot for casual meetups, family gatherings, or intimate dates.

The open-air rooftop setting provides a refreshing escape from the everyday pace, while the diverse menu caters to both comfort food lovers and adventurous diners. From hearty grilled dishes and homestyle pasta to specialty coffee drinks and indulgent desserts — there's something for everyone.

Whether you're starting your morning with a perfectly crafted latte or ending your evening with friends over Beef Stroganoff and carrot cake, PAN GRILL delivers flavor, ambiance, and warmth in every visit.
